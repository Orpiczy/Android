Ch01Ex01 - Customizing the Toast

In this challenge, you will customize the toast to show at the top
instead of the bottom of the screen. To change how the toast is displayed,\
use the Toast class’s setGravity method. Use Gravity.TOP for the gravity value.
Refer to the developer documentation at
developer.android.com/reference/android/widget/Toast.html#setGravity(int,int,int)
for more details.
